The Staff

Richard Crosby

Richard originally started Crosby Financial Services CC (CFS) with Yvonne in 1991 and then went back into the Corporate world as the Financoial Director and 2 IC at The South African Futures Exchange (SAFEX). This left Yvonne to manage the business until Richard returned full-time in November 2001. Richard is a CA (SA) and a member of SAICA and until recently was a Registered Auditor. He also qualified as a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) in 2003.

Yvonne Crosby

As mentioned above Yvonne has managed CFS since it was formed in 1991 having had a successful career as the Manager of a Shopping Centre on the West Rand. She now assists wherever necessary and actively markets the organisation.

Nancy Kehlert

Nancy has been with the Group since July 2005 and is the proverbial mouthpiece of the organisation. She started by with tax and accounting duties and is our Payroll administrator. She now however assists Richard with the services offered via Richplan and has been persuaded to study further in the financial planning field.

Suzette Botha

Suzette joined the Group in April 2009 and has nearly completed her Accounting.. She handles the processing of records for all our VAT clients and assists with the tax and financial statement for a number of clients. She also administers any deceased estates that we need to finalise.
